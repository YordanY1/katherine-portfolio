About Me

Graphic design student with a strong interest in visual communication, branding and meaningful design.

Katerina Stoimenova

My name is Katerina Stoimenova, and I am currently studying graphic design while actively developing my skills in the field of visual communication.

I am motivated to grow professionally as a designer, with a strong focus on creating projects that are not only visually appealing, but also functional and meaningful.

I approach every project with discipline, attention to detail and a strong sense of responsibility. I consider myself a consistent and dedicated person, always eager to learn new techniques and tools in order to improve and evolve.

I strive to develop in graphic design because I believe it is a field that combines creativity and communication, with the power to build recognizable and impactful visual messages.
